    Our Services

    Document Registration

    It deals with recording of documents with the respected officer and to make it secure by keeping orginal copies.It can be in different areas, starting from sales deeds, property registrations,land registration etc to many.Sales deed is a legal and approved written document which is used to prove that the sale is completed.It also includes property and land registrations which stands as a legal proof for showing the owner change of a registered property and land.Legal agreement is major part of document registration, which is a written document that is registered legally to identify the parties, roles and responsibilities written under the agreement.

    Firm Registration

    Firm is a group of professionals who need to work together in same area under same roof. For establishing this concept we need some legal registrations. It includes many areas such as company registration, trust registration, partnership firm registration, association registration, society registration, family association registration etc. company registration deals with registering a profitable business organisation in other hand trust registration, society registration , family registration etc is a legal registration of a group of people, which is non-profitable. Another registration is partnership firm registration which gives a proof of partners, roles, clauses and responsibilities.

    Criminal Law Services

    It has three classification in our constitution, i.e infraction, misdemeanors and felony. This all deals with different types.The infraction consists of minor cases such as speeding while driving, disobeying seat belt laws, violation of building codes etc while in the case of misdemeanors, the crime level of severity is little more higher, i.e it contains the cases like Harrasment, trespassing, public intoxication etc. The another category of felony, which deals with higher severity cases having the most severe penalities, rape,murder, drug abuse violation etc.

    Civil Law Services

    It deals with behaviour that constitutes an injury to an individual or other private property.cases such as drunken driving, theft, assault etc.

    Family Law Services

    We have solutions for family related cases such as divorce, disorderly misconduct etc. All family related issues comes under this.

    Property Conversation

    It deals with conversion of property, that is from one purpose to another. For making this conversion legal, registration is needed.
